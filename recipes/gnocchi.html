Gnocchi

How to make Gnocchi

Gnocchi ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Preparation step by step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Congratulations! Your gnocchi is ready!
